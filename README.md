# OneDayIntradayReversal

## Overview

This repository contains the research and implementation of a systematic **One-Day Intraday Reversal (ODIR)** trading strategy.

The objective is not only to evaluate the profitability of the strategy across a broad universe of assets, but also to identify the statistical characteristics that explain why some assets are naturally more receptive to an intraday reversal signal than others.

The project combines quantitative research, feature engineering and statistical analysis.

## Research Roadmap

The research is organized into four main parts:

1. Evaluate the unconditional performance of the strategy across the full universe.
2. Identify the market conditions and asset characteristics associated with high performance.
3. Build a statistical model to select the most suitable assets.
4. Validate the selection methodology out-of-sample.
